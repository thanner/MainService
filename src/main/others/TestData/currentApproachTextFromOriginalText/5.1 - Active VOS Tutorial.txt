The process starts when the customer receives a customer request for a loan amount. Next, he invokes the risk assessment to assess the request. Subsequently, one of the 3 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the customer conducts the customer is low risk and then approves the loan. In the second procedure, if required, the customer conducts the loan amount is for $10,000 or more. In any case, the customer sends the request to the approver web service and then receives the feedback. In the third procedure, the customer denies the loan. In any case, the process ends.
