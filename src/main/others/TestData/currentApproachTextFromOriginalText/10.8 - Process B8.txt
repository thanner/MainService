The process starts when the mspo conducts a deregisters at the go. Next, the go verifies the deregistration. Subsequently, one of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the go confirms the deregistration of the mspo. In the second procedure, the go rejects the deregistration of the mspo. In any case, the go assigns the deregistration of the mspo and then informs the mspo about end of the assignment. After that, it informs the mpo about assignment of the go and then informs the sp about assignment of the go. Finally, the process ends.
