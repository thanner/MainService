The process starts when the Resource 1 occurs an once to set down the matter. Next, one of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the Resource 1 contacts one week to determine if they are all ready to go to trial. In the second procedure, the Resource 1 sets the callover. In any case, the Resource 1 defers the callover to the next available slot and then prepares the callover material. Subsequently, it holds the callover. Finally, the process ends.
