The process starts when the ec tells an inq about change of the master data. Next, the inq notifies the ip of the change and then the ip checks. Subsequently, one of the 1 alternative procedures is executed. The ip waits up to the time limit of changes the master data at the desired time. After that, one of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the ip confirms the changes of the inq. In the second procedure, the ip rejects the changes of the inq. In any case, the process ends.
