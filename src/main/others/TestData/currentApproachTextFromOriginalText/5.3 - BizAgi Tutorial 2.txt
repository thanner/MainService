The process starts when the employee of the organization submits an office supply request. Next, it registers the requirement and then the immediate supervisor of the employee receives the request to request the office supplies. Subsequently, one of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the supervisor asks the request. After that, if required, the supervisor returns the request to the petitioner\/employee. In the second procedure, the supervisor approves the request and then goes the quotations to the purchase department. Next, he selects a vendor. Subsequently, if required, the purchase department chooses a different vendor. After that, the purchase department selects a vendor and then confirms a vendor. Next, it generates a purchase order and then sends a purchase order. Subsequently, it conducts the to be deliver and then receives. After that, it sends a notification to let what the result was in case. Next, one of the 3 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the purchase department requires. In the second procedure, the purchase department requires. In the third procedure, the purchase department requires. In any case, the purchase department sends a notification. In any case, the process ends.
