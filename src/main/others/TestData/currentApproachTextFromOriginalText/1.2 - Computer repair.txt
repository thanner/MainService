The process starts when the customer brings in a defective computer. Next, the crs checks the defect back and then checks the hands back. Subsequently, if required, the customer takes the computer home unrepaired. In any case, the customer consists of the two activities. After that, one of the 4 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the customer repairs the hardware whereas second activity checks and then tests the proper system functionality. In the second procedure, the customer checks the hardware. In the third procedure, the customer finishes the repair. In the fourth procedure, the customer executes the arbitrary repair activity. In any case, the process ends.
