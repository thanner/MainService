The process starts when the party sends a possession request asking a warrant to the be released. Next, he conducts the to be release and then the client service back office receives the request. Subsequently, the client service back office retrieves the sct file and then forwards the sct warrant possession to the queensland police. After that, it stores the physical sct file to await a report to sent and then the police conducts the to be send. Next, the police receives the report and then retrieves the respective sct file. Subsequently, the office attaches the new sct document and then attaches the stores. After that, the other internal mc staff receives the physical sct file. Finally, the process ends.
