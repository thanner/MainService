The process starts when the customer submits a claim. Next, he sends and then the notification department checks the documents. Subsequently, the handling department picks up the claim and then picks up the checks. After that, it performs the assessment. Next, one of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the handling department phones a garage to authorise the repairs and then schedules the payment. In the second procedure, the handling department rejects the claim. In any case, the handling department sends a letter to the customer and then considers the process to the complete. Finally, the process ends.
