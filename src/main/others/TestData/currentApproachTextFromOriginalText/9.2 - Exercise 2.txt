The process starts when the customer service representative sends a mortgage offer to the customer. Next, it waits for a reply. Subsequently, if required, the customer writes the back to decline the mortgage. In any case, the customer updates the case details and then archives the work. After that, one of the 3 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the customer waits up to the time limit of receives no answer after 2 weeks and then updates the case details. In the second procedure, the customer generates a message to request outstanding documents to the customer. In the third procedure, the customer sends a message and then attaches the prerequisite documents. Next, he moves the case to the administration. In any case, the process ends.
