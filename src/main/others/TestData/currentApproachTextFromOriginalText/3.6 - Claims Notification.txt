The process starts when the organization receives a claim. Next, it checks a claim. Subsequently, if required, one of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the organization evaluates the severity of the claim. In the second procedure, the organization informs the claimant. In any case, the organization sends the relevant forms to the claimant and then returns forms. After that, it checks the severity of the claim. Next, one of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the organization registers the claim in claims management system. In the second procedure, the organization informs the claimant to update the forms. In any case, the organization checks the claimant again. In any case, the process ends.
