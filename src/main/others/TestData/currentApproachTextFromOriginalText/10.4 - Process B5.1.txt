The process starts when the mpon notifies the mpoo about equipment change intentions. Next, one of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the mpoo announces the self. In the second procedure, the mpoo notifies the mpon about no self-dismounting of the mpoo. In any case, one of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the mpoo performs the final reading. In the second procedure, the mpon performs the final reading. In any case, one of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the mpon dismounts the old equipment. In the second procedure, the mpoo dismounts the old equipment. In any case, the mpon mounts the new device and then reads the meter count. Subsequently, it sends the values of final reading and then tells the go about device changes. After that, the go notifies the msp about device changes. Finally, the process ends.
