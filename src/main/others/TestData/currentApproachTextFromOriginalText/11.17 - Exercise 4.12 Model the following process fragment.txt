The process starts when the supplier confirms a wholesale order. Next, he transmits the order. Subsequently, if required, the supplier needs to compute the route plan a full track-load half track-load in order prepare. After that, the supplier prepares the quote. Next, if required, the supplier needs to estimate the trailer usage a full track-load half track-load in order prepare. Subsequently, the supplier dispatches within the four days and then implies that the transportation quotes have to be prepared within 48 hours from the receipt of the order to remain within the terms of the contract. In any case, the process ends.
