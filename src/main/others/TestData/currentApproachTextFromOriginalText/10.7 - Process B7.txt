The process starts when the mspn registers a measurement. Next, the go examines the application of the mspn. Subsequently, one of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the go confirms the application of the mspn. In the second procedure, the go rejects the application of the mspn. In any case, the go assigns the mspn and then informs the mspo about assignment of the mspn. After that, it informs the mpo about assignment of the mspn and then informs the sp about assignment of the mspn. Finally, the process ends.
