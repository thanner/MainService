The process starts when the Notification department checks a documentation. Next, it registers the claim and then the Handling department checks the insurance. Subsequently, the Handling department performs the assessment. After that, one of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the Handling department arranges the repair and then schedules the payment. In the second procedure, the Handling department rejects the claim. In any case, the Handling department notifies the customer. Finally, the process ends.
