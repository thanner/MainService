Once a message is received the process starts. Next, the Hotel takes down the order. Subsequently, 3 procedures are executed in an arbitrary order. The Hotel assigns the order to the waiter. After that, 2 procedures are executed in an arbitrary order. The Hotel readies the cart. In the meantime, if required, the Hotel prepares the non-alcoholic drinks. At the same time, the Hotel submits the order ticket to the kitchen and then prepares the meal. In the meantime, if required, the Hotel gives the order to the sommelier. After each case, the Hotel delivers to the guest's room and then returns to the room-service station. Next, it debits the guest's account. Subsequently, if required, sends a message. In any case, the process ends.
