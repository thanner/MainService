Once a message is received the process starts. Next, one of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the Resource 1 checks the facts presented. In the second procedure, the Resource 1 conducts the activity. The subprocess contains the following steps. One of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the Resource 1 prepares the registration statement for the approval of sec and then receives a message. In the second procedure, the Resource 1 checks the compliance with the laws. In any case, the Resource 1 talks to the potential investors and then receives a message. Subsequently, if required, the Resource 1 fixes the issue price. After that, the Resource 1 buys the stock and then offers to the public. In any case, the subprocess ends and then the Resource 1 sends a message. If it is required, the latter steps are repeated. Next, the process continues. In any case, the process ends.
