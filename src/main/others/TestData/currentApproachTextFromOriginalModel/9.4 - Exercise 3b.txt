Once a message is received the process starts. Next, the Resource 1 meetings the takes place and then receives a message. Finally, the process ends. Once a time condition is fulfilled the process starts. Next, the Support Officer creates the meeting folder and then uploads all relevant documents. Subsequently, it waits up to the time limit of 1 week before meeting and then sends a message. After that, it attendses the meeting and then prepares the meeting minutes. Next, the Coordination Unit conducts the qa check and then the Support Officer reminds the coordination unit. If it is required, the latter steps are repeated. In that case the Coordination Unit continues with conducting the qa check. Subsequently, the Support Officer sends a message. Finally, the process ends.
