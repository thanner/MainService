The process starts when the Resource 1 checks the customer details. Next, one of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the Resource 1 rejects the claim. In any case, the Resource 1 evaluates the severity and then sends the relevant forms. Subsequently, it checks for the completeness. As long as is the Resource 1 repeats the latter steps and continues with sending the relevant forms. After that, the process continues. Next, the Resource 1 registers the claim. In any case, the process ends.
