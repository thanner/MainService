When the process starts, one of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the Resource 1 causeses a change by the itself. In the second procedure, the Resource 1 requests a change. In any case, the Resource 1 reviews. Next, one of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the Resource 1 conducts the rejection of the change. In the second procedure, the Resource 1 conducts the confirmation of the request and then conducts the perfomance of the measuring point change. Subsequently, one of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the Resource 1 conducts the implementation report. In the second procedure, the Resource 1 conducts the failure of the changes. In any case, the process ends.
