The process starts when the Resource 1 conducts a registration. Next, it sends a message and then receives a message. Finally, the process ends. Once a message is received the process starts. Next, the Resource 2 conducts the examination. Subsequently, one of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the Resource 2 conducts the confirmation of registration and then conducts the assingment of the mspn. After that, it conducts the information about the assingment of the mspn and then conducts the information about the assingment of the mspn. Next, it conducts the information about the assingment of the mspn. In the second procedure, the Resource 2 conducts the rejection of registration. In any case, the process ends.
