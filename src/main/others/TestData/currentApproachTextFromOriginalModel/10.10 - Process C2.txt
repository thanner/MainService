When the process starts, one of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the Resource 1 conducts the suspicion of fault. In the second procedure, the Resource 1 receives a message. In any case, the Resource 1 examines the failure report. Next, one of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the Resource 1 conducts the rejection of failure report. In the second procedure, the Resource 1 conducts the confirmation of failure report. In any case, the Resource 1 informs the go about failure and then informs the msp about failure. Subsequently, it repairs of the fault and then conducts the information about the results of the repair or the fault. After that, it conducts the information about the resolution of the interference and then conducts the information about the resolution of the interference. Finally, the process ends. The process starts when the Resource 2 reports of a failure. Next, it sends a message and then receives a message. Finally, the process ends.
