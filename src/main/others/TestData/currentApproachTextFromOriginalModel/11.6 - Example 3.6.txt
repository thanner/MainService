The process starts when the Resource 1 checks a stock availability. Next, one of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. If "product in stock", the Resource 1 retrieves the product from the warehouse. Afterwards, the Resource 1 checks the raw materials availability and then manufactures the product. In any case, the Resource 1 confirms the order. Subsequently, 2 procedures are executed in an arbitrary order. The Resource 1 gets the shipment address and then ships the product. In the meantime, the Resource 1 emits the invoice and then receives the payment. After each case, the Resource 1 archives the order. Finally, the process ends.
