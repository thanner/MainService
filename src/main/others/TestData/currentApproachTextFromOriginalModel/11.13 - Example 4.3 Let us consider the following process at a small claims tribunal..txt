Once a time condition is fulfilled the process starts. Next, the Resource 1 prepares the callover list and then waits up to the time limit of 1 week prior to callover day. Subsequently, it contacts the parties. After that, one of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. If "not all parties agree", the Resource 1 defers the callover. If "all parties agree", the Resource 1 sets the callover and then waits up to the time limit of callover day. Next, it prepares the callover material and then holds the callover. In any case, the process ends.
