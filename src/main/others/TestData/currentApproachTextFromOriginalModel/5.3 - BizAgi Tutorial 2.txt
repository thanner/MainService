When the process starts, one of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. The Purchase Department processes the invoice. In the second procedure, the Employee registers the office supply request and then the Boss approves the office supply request. Next, one of the 3 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the Employee notifies the change required. In the second procedure, the Purchase Department notifies the approval and then conducts the quotations. Subsequently, it generates the purchase order and then the Employee receives the products. After that, the Employee sends a signal. In the third procedure, the Boss notifies the rejection. In any case, the process ends.
