The process starts when the Resource 1 conducts a deregistration. Next, it sends a message and then receives a message. Finally, the process ends. Once a message is received the process starts. Next, one of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the Resource 2 conducts the preliminary confirmation of the deregistration and then assigns the go as msp. Subsequently, it conducts the information about the new assignment and then conducts the information about the new assignment. After that, it conducts the information about the new assignment. In the second procedure, the Resource 2 conducts the rejection of the deregistration. In any case, the process ends.
