Once a message is received the process starts. Next, one of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the Resource 1 waits up to the time limit of 7 days and then conducts the approval in progress email. Subsequently, it waits up to the time limit of 23 days and then advises the employee to the start again. In the second procedure, if required, the Resource 1 creates the account. In any case, the Resource 1 reviews for the pre-approval. After that, one of the 3 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the Resource 1 sends for the payment and then transfers to the employee account. In the second procedure, the Resource 1 conducts the supervisor review and then sends for the payment. Next, it transfers to the employee account. In any case, the process ends.
