The process starts when the Customer Service Representative sends out a mortgage offer. Next, one of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. The Customer Service Representative receives a message and then the Administration assesses the mortgage details. Subsequently, one of the following alternative procedures is performed. One of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. The Customer Service Representative receives a message. The Customer Service Representative waits up to the time limit of 2 weeks. In any case, the Customer Service Representative updates the case file and archives. In any case, the process ends.
