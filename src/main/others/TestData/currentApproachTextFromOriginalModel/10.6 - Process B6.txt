The process starts when the Resource 1 conducts a dismissal. Next, it sends a message and then receives a message. Finally, the process ends. Once a message is received the process starts. Next, the Resource 2 reviews. Subsequently, one of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. In the first procedure, the Resource 2 conducts the rejection of dismissal. In the second procedure, the Resource 2 conducts the confirmation of dismissal. In any case, the process ends.
