The process starts when the Marketing conducts an activity. The subprocess contains the following steps. One of the 3 alternative procedures is executed. If "(45%)", the Marketing conducts the other sources. If "(30%)", the Marketing buys the data. If "(25%)", the Marketing visits the exhibition and then imports the excel-sheet with the customer data. In any case, the subprocess ends. Next, the Call-Center Employee determines the contact person. Subsequently, if required, the Call-Center Employee determines the available project budget. After that, one of the 2 alternative procedures is executed. If "not interested (small budget) (20%)", the Call-Center Employee remembers the customer for the follow-up. In any case, the process ends.
